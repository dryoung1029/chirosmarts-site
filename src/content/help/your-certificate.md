## How it's issued

When you finish a course — meeting the seat-time requirement and passing the
required quizzes and final exam — your certificate is **generated automatically**
and **emailed to you** as a PDF. It's also saved to your account.

## What's on it

- Your **legal name**
- The **course title** and **credit hours**
- Your **completion date**
- The **instructor** of record
- A unique **verification code**

These values are **locked in at the moment of issuance**, so your certificate is
a permanent, accurate record.

## Verifying a certificate

Every certificate has a public **verification code**. Anyone — an employer, the
board — can confirm it's genuine at the verification page, no login required.
This is how a clinic or auditor checks that a certificate is real and unaltered.

## Wrong name on your certificate?

Your certificate uses your **legal name** from your profile. If it's wrong,
**[contact support](/help/contact)** — certificates can be reissued with the
corrected information.
