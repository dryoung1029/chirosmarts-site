A **seat** lets you grant one CA access to one course. Seats are organized into a
**pool per course**, so you buy seats for the specific training you need.

## Buying seats

1. Open your **Clinic** page.
2. Under **Buy training seats**, pick the course and the number of seats.
3. Confirm. [VERIFY: confirm pricing and whether checkout is live or test-mode
   comped.]

Each course you buy seats for gets its own pool, showing how many seats are
**available**, **in use**, and **purchased**.

## Assigning a seat to a CA

1. In that course's pool, enter the CA's **email** and click **Assign seat**.
2. The CA gets an **invite** to claim their seat (see
   **[Inviting and tracking CAs](/help/inviting-and-tracking-cas)**).
3. Once they join, the seat shows as **in use** and they have course access.

## Running low

If a pool has **no seats available**, the assign button is disabled — just buy
more seats for that course and continue assigning.

> One seat = one CA's access to one course. To train a CA in two courses, you'll
> assign them a seat from each course's pool.
