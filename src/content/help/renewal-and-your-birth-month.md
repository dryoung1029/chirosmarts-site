Once you're a certified CA, you keep your certification current with **annual
continuing education (CE)**.

## When it's due

Your renewal is due on the **last day of your birth month**, and each year
requires **6 hours of continuing education** (including 1 hour of cultural
competency and 2 hours of vitals training). The platform shows your renewal
timing on your dashboard so you're never guessing.

## How to renew

1. From the catalog, enroll in the **renewal** course.
2. Complete it the same way as any course — watch the lessons, meet seat time,
   pass the quiz.
3. Your renewal **certificate** is issued and emailed automatically.

## Not sure if you're due?

Use the **renewal-date checker** on the public site, or open your dashboard —
your birth-month renewal note is shown there. Questions about your specific
deadline? **[Contact support](/help/contact)**.
