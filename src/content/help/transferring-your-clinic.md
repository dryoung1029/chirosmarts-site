When you leave or change roles, you can hand the clinic to a new manager. The
transfer takes effect **immediately**.

## How to transfer

1. Open your **Clinic** page and find **Transfer clinic manager**.
2. Choose the new manager one of two ways:
   - **Promote one of your CAs** who has already joined, or
   - **Hand off to any email.** If they don't have an account yet, we create one
     and email them a sign-in link.
3. Confirm.

## What happens

- The new manager gets **full access** to seats and CAs.
- You become a **regular account** — you keep your own training, but you no longer
  manage the clinic.
- Your seats, pools, and CA assignments stay intact and move with the clinic.

> Transfer is immediate and hands over full control, so double-check the email
> before confirming. If you transfer by mistake, the new manager can transfer it
> right back to you.

Questions before transferring? **[Contact support](/help/contact)** first.
