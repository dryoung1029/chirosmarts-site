ChiroSmarts is an online training and compliance platform for Oregon
Chiropractic Assistants (CAs). It handles two things:

- **Initial certification** — the state-required training to become a certified CA.
- **Annual renewal** — the continuing-education (CE) you take each year to stay current.

## Your dashboard

After you sign in, your **dashboard** shows two things:

1. **Your courses** — anything you're enrolled in, with a link to keep watching.
2. **Your roadmap** — a step-by-step checklist (create your account → finish the
   course → exam → certified) so you always know what's next.

## How you make progress

Courses are made of short video lessons. As you watch, the platform records how
much of each lesson's content you've actually covered (your **seat time**). Once
you've watched enough of every lesson, your **final exam** unlocks. Pass it, and
your **certificate** is generated automatically and emailed to you.

## Running a clinic?

If you manage a clinic, you can buy a pool of training seats and assign them to
your CAs, then track each person to certification. See
**[Setting up your clinic](/help/setting-up-your-clinic)**.

Still stuck? **[Contact support](/help/contact)** any time.
