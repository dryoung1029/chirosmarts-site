ChiroSmarts has **no passwords**. Instead, you sign in with a one-time link we
email you (a "magic link").

## How to sign in

1. Go to the **sign-in page** and enter your email address.
2. We email you a link. Open the email and click **Sign in**.
3. You're in. The link works once and expires after **15 minutes**.

## Tips & troubleshooting

- **Didn't get the email?** Check spam, and make sure you typed the address
  correctly. You can request a new link any time — it simply replaces the old one.
- **Link expired or already used?** Just request a fresh one from the sign-in page.
- **Use the same email every time.** Your courses and progress are tied to the
  exact address you signed up with. `you@example.com` and `you+ca@example.com`
  are treated as **different accounts**.
- **Staying signed in.** You can be signed in on more than one device. For
  security, only **one device can play video at a time** — starting playback on
  a new device will take over from the old one.

If you're locked out and a new link still isn't arriving,
**[contact support](/help/contact)** and we'll help.
