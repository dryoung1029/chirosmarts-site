The final exam stays locked until you've genuinely completed the course. Two
conditions must **both** be met:

1. **Watch ≥ 90% of every lesson.** There's no skipping — each individual lesson
   needs at least 90% of its content covered. One unwatched lesson keeps the exam
   locked.
2. **Reach the course's required minutes.** Some courses set a minimum total of
   content-minutes you must accrue; your dashboard shows that target and your
   progress toward it (many courses have no extra floor beyond the 90% rule).

## How to tell what's left

Open the course from your dashboard. Each lesson shows its progress, so you can
see which ones still need watching. Work through anything below 90% and the exam
will unlock on its own — there's no button to press.

## Why it's strict

This mirrors the state's no-skip, content-based requirement. It protects the
value of your certificate: everyone who holds one has actually completed the
training.

> **Note:** Credit hours on your certificate are set by the course and may differ
> from the video length (for example, when credit includes off-video practice).
> The exam gate is about content watched; the certificate figure is separate.

Stuck below 90% on a lesson you've watched? **[Contact support](/help/contact)**
with the course and lesson name and we'll look into it.
