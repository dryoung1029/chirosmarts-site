## How an invite works

When you assign a seat to a CA's email, they receive an **invite link**. Clicking
it signs them in (no password) and enrolls them in the course — claiming the seat
also proves they own that email address.

## Reading the statuses

On your Clinic page, each assigned CA shows a status:

- **Invited** — the invite was sent but they haven't claimed it yet. You can
  **revoke** an unclaimed invite to free the seat.
- **Joined** — they claimed the seat and are finishing their intake.
- **In training** — they've onboarded and are working through the course.

## Tracking progress

Your Clinic page lists every CA per course so you can see who's invited, who's
joined, and who's underway. Follow up with anyone still showing **Invited** if
they haven't claimed their seat.

## Did an invite not arrive?

Ask the CA to check spam and confirm the email address you used. You can revoke
and re-assign to resend. Still stuck? **[Contact support](/help/contact)**.
