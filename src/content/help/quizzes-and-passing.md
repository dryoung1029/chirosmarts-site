## Passing score

Quizzes and the final exam have a passing threshold — **80% by default**, though
a course may set its own. [VERIFY: confirm per-course pass thresholds.] You'll see
your score immediately after submitting.

## Retakes

- If you don't pass, you can **try again**. Every attempt is **kept on record** —
  we never erase a failed attempt — but you only need one passing attempt to move
  forward.
- After a graded quiz, you can **jump straight to the video moment** that covers a
  question you missed, so you can review before retrying.

## The final exam

The final exam works the same way, and unlocks only after you've met the
[seat-time and lesson requirements](/help/unlocking-the-final-exam). Passing the
final exam (and any required course quizzes) is what triggers your
**[certificate](/help/your-certificate)**.

Having trouble submitting a quiz? **[Contact support](/help/contact)**.
