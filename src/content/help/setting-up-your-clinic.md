A **clinic account** lets you train your Chiropractic Assistants in one place:
buy a pool of training seats, assign them to your CAs, and track each person to
certification.

## Becoming a clinic manager

You become a clinic manager when you sign up and choose **"Clinic owner /
manager"** during intake, entering your clinic's name. That creates your clinic
and gives you access to the **Clinic** area.

## Two separate screens

Your account keeps clinic work and your own training apart:

- **Dashboard** — your personal training (any courses *you* take).
- **Clinic** — everything for managing CAs: seats, invites, and progress. Reach
  it from the **Clinic** link in the top navigation.

This separation means clinic management never clutters your training view, and
vice-versa.
